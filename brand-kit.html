Sample Brand Narrative

Terramonga Hub sources local food from family farms across Western North Carolina and delivers to your home each week. Joe and Elaina Sabbag founded Terramonga Farm in Fairview in 2018, starting with vegetables and later adding sheep, chickens, pigs, and cattle. We believe in the unique power of food hubs to aggregate and distribute local food and connect our community with their local food sources.
